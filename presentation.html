Approach & Methodology

1

Data Cleaning & Prep

Handled categorical variables (One-Hot Encoding) for 'Department' and 'JobRole'. Checked for class imbalance (Attrition 'Yes' is minority class).

2

Modeling Strategy

Moved from basic correlation matrices to Logistic Regression. This allowed me to isolate the coefficient weights of each feature to understand directionality.

3

Diagnostic Analytics

Distinguished between Risk Accelerators (drivers of exit) and Protective Factors (anchors of retention).
